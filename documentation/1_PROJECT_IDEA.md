# DocuDrop: Project Concept

## The Vision
DocuDrop is designed as a high-fidelity, privacy-centric "Digital Wallet" for your most important documents. Unlike cloud-based storage solutions, DocuDrop focuses on local-first persistence and a premium user experience inspired by physical card holders and travel pass managers.

The core philosophy is **"Aesthetic Utility"**: the app should not only be a tool for viewing documents (PDFs and Images) but should provide a tactile, visually satisfying interface that makes managing digital passes feel like interacting with a physical object.

## Core Values

1. **Privacy First**: All documents are stored locally on the device's secure storage. There is no cloud syncing, no accounts, and no tracking. Your documents never leave your device unless you explicitly export a backup.
2. **Mobile Optimization**: While built with web technologies, the app is architected specifically for Android via Capacitor, ensuring native-like performance and integration.
3. **Tactile UI**: The "Stacked Wallet" look isn't just for show; it provides a compact way to browse dozens of items while maintaining quick access through search and metadata visibility.

## Target Use Cases
- **Travel**: Boarding passes, hotel bookings, and transit tickets.
- **Identify**: Digital copies of IDs, licenses, and insurance cards.
- **Events**: Concert tickets, membership cards, and QR-based passes.
- **Daily Life**: Receipts, prescriptions, or any quick-reference document.

## The Name
**DocuDrop** represents the ease of the "Drop" action—simply selecting a file and dropping it into your secure digital vault.
